A Crash Course in Formula One




Formula One is the highest class of international racing class of international racing for open-wheel, single-seater formula racing cars sanctioned by the Fédération Internationale de l'Automobile. The FIA Formula One World Championship has been one of the premier forms of racing around the world since its inaugural season in 1950.
